Welcome

Welcome to the Online Vegetable Sales and Marketing Information System. This platform helps farmers, vendors, and customers manage vegetable products, orders, and payments efficiently. The system was developed after identifying a major challenge faced by farmers dealing in perishable goods. Many farmers travel from villages to urban markets with the hope of finding buyers for their products. However, they often face uncertainty because there is no guarantee that their produce will be purchased. As a result, farmers may waste fuel, time, and other resources, or return home with unsold goods that can easily spoil, leading to financial losses.

About the System

The Online Vegetable Sales and Marketing Information System is designed to connect farmers, vendors, and customers through an easy-to-use online platform.Through the system, farmers can register and upload information about their products, while vendors can search for available produce and place orders directly with farmers. The system therefore improves communication between both parties, reduces losses caused by unsold produce, saves time, and promotes a more efficient and transparent trading process for farm products.

Features:

  • Farmer registration
  • Vendor registration
  • Vegetable products
  • Customer orders
  • Payment processing
